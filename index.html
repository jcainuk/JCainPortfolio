About Me

Hello. My name is Jonathan Cain. I'm a junior web developer and a graduate from the Manchester Codes web development bootcamp, a coding school based in Manchester. This was an intensive course covering Test-Driven-Development/TDD, JavaScript, Express, MySQL, React, Node.js, HTML & CSS. I've been hooked on web development ever since I typed my first line of code. At present I'm continuing to build on my knowledge using courses on Udemy.

My background is in teaching English as a foreign language to adults. I have years of experience of standing up and presenting in front of diverse groups of students so I'm good at breaking down difficult concepts into clear simple language. Collaborating and working as a team is something I really enjoy. When I'm not coding I enjoy yoga, reading, gaming and sightseeing.
