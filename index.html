About Me

Hello. My name is Jonathan Cain. I am a graduate from the July 2023 cohort of Northcoders, a 13 week full-time software engineering bootcamp.

Leveraging my background as a former English as a foreign language teacher, I bring strong communication skills to collaborative settings. I have hands-on experience with Test-Driven Development, Pair-Programming, OOP, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL and MongoDB. I'm committed to maintaining high coding standards. I approach problem-solving with diligence and am eager to contribute my growing technical expertise to meaningful projects. With a genuine passion for coding, I'm dedicated to lifelong learning and making valuable contributions within a team.

I have years of experience of standing up and presenting in front of diverse groups of students so I'm good at breaking down difficult concepts into clear and simple language. Collaborating and working as a team is something I really enjoy. When I'm not coding I enjoy yoga, reading, gaming and sightseeing.
